StackedWordTrees:
	1.	A StackedWordTree is a sequence of words, starting with a 3-letter word, that are incrementally longer than each other via the addition of a letter
	2.	Example of a StackedWordTree:
	1.	bus
	2.	stub
	3.	burst
	4.	bursts
	3.	All words in a StackedWordTree must appear in the input wordlist
